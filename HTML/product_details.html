Blessing Apsara

$600.00
Year: 2021
Type: Oil on Canvas
Size: 62x82cm
Artist: DinArt

Description

Blessing Apsara is the Oil on Canvas artwork created in 2021 by the artist DinArt. This painting is inspired by the beautiful culture of Cambodia. There is a traditional dance in Cambodia call "Blessing Dance" which is often performed by a group of dancer to give the blessing in many events such as weddings, opening ceremony, etc. Taking the inspiration from this beautiful dance, this artwork showcase a part of the dance.
