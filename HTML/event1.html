From Funan to Present • Exhibition

SOSORO Museum, Phnom Penh, Cambodia

Open from Tuesday to Sunday, 9am - 6pm

2,000 years of Cambodia’s Monetary History ....

This permanent exhibition highlights some fundamental facts and lessons learned from the country's economic and monetary history. They highlight some fundamental facts and lessons learned from the country’s economic and monetary history and focus on the strong interaction between money, credit, growth, inflation, etc. Visitors can discover at their own pace 2,000 years of Cambodia’s history through a large number of display cases and panels enhanced by multimedia presentations

1st-15th century • From Funan to Angkor

1431-1953 • From Kingdoms to Protectorate

1953-1998 • Independence, Wars, and reconstruction
